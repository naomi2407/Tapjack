/Responsive/

A game by Naomi Shah, Shikhar Juyal and Veda Adnani

In this interactive experiment, a game of ‘Tapjack’ challenges your reaction skills to a series of fast-paced flashing images. Two teams of ten players are provided with a primary image and a console of 10 mobile screens. As a series of images keep flashing by, players have to identify the correct images that form part of the primary image, similar to a puzzle.
